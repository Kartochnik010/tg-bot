# lib_time 
Библиотека для работы с датой и временем

## v2
- go 1.21
- DateTime Layouts
- StdFormat (like dd/mm/yyyy)

StdFormat placeholders
--------------------------
	YYYY - 2006
	YY - 22

	MMMM - January
	MMM - Jan
	MM - 01
	M - 1

	DDDD - Monday
	DDD - Mon
	DD - 02
	D - 2

	hh - 15
	h - 3
	am/pm - PM

	mm - 04
	m - 4

	ss - 05
	s - 5
	.s - .99

	zzzz - Z07:00:00
	zzz - Z0700
	zz - Z07:00
	z - Z07
